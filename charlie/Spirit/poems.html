Buddhist Resources In or Near Santa Cruz

There are many resources in the Bay area. However, I will focus on a few local retreat centers, and then some places to practice here in Santa Cruz. Feel free to drop in to any of the local meditation meetings at Vipassana Santa Cruz or Everyday Dharam, and you can visit the Zen Center on Wednesday when they have an introduction to their practice.

Local Retreat Centers:

  • Spirit Rock in Marin County is a Vipassana retreat center, the home of Jack Kornfield.
  • Shambhala is another Zen practice with centers in the Bay area. Pema Chodron works in this tradition.
  • Green Gulch in Marin Country is associated with the San Francisco Zen Center
  • Tassajara near Big Sur is associated with the San Francisco Zen Center.

Everyday Dharma (Carolyn Atkinson) 

http://www.everydaydharma.org/

Everyday Dharma Weekly Meditation Sittings

13 New Street in Downtown Santa Cruz
Each Wednesday at 7 P.M.

Sitting meditation begins at 7:00 PM and is 40 minutes long. Carolyn Atkinson, our Head Teacher, then gives a Dharma Talk, which usually lasts half an hour.

Then there is a discussion of the Dharma Talk, followed by announcements.

After chanting the Bodhisattva Vows or doing a brief practice in Metta meditation, we bow out. We then gather outside for tea and conversation.

EveryDay Dharma Daily Meditation Sittings

New Street in downtown Santa Cruz. Every Day, Noon to 12:40 PM. ---What to do in downtown Santa Cruz at Noon--- A quiet alternative.

The zendo is open for meditation seven days a week at twelve noon. Sitting meditation is 40 minutes long, with opening and closing bells. No Dharma Talk is given. A quiet time to just sit down in the middle of our lives. . .
